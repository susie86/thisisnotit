This Is Not It

Launched in 2017, This is not it is a blog that explores art and other aspects of visual culture. Each week you’ll find 5 posts on photography, design, animation, painting, installation art, architecture, drawing, and street art.

About Us

This is not it began as the personal art and design blog of Susan Sayers before growing somewhat unexpectedly into what you see today.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring a diverse range of visual culture to the masses.
